About FreshCo Mushroom: Cultivating Trust Since 2005

Welcome to FreshCo Mushroom, where our commitment to freshness and quality has been unwavering since 2005. Our journey began with the vision of our esteemed founder, Ramdas Verma, who aimed to bring the freshest, most vibrant mushrooms to every table. Soon after, co-founder Basant Lal joined, solidifying our foundation and enriching our pursuit of excellence.

Together, they built a company now trusted by over 50,000 customers. This remarkable milestone is a testament to our dedication to meticulous quality control and sustainable practices. In 2020, we proudly formalized our operations by completing our official company registration, further reinforcing our commitment to professionalism and transparency. As a fully GST-registered company, we uphold the highest standards of integrity in all our dealings.

At FreshCo Mushroom, we cultivate more than just mushrooms; we cultivate lasting relationships based on quality, trust, and customer satisfaction. We strive to ensure that the freshness you expect from us remains unparalleled, bringing nature's best directly to you.
